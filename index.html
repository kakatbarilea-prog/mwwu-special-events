The Boys Are Back In Town

Date and time

September 13, 2025 · 06:30 PM

Location

Make Wine With Us Facility — 21 Curie Ave, Wallington, NJ 07057
Get directions ↗

Make Wine With Us in Wallington, NJ is excited to present The Boys Are Back In Town on Friday, September 13th at 6:30 PM — a throwback musical experience you won’t want to miss.

Enjoy live performances by Phil Miuccio, the King of Swing, and Jerry Lanzerotti, the King of Doo-Wop, as they bring timeless classics from the Rat Pack era and the golden hits of the 50s and 60s. Whether you're a fan of smooth crooning or doo-wop harmonies, this evening is designed to get you dancing.

To keep the energy going, AE Entertainment will be spinning extended dance tracks after the show. Feel free to bring your own food and drinks — and your dancing shoes! Tickets are $30 per person, and proceeds benefit the American Wine Society Education Foundation, supporting wine education and research nationwide.

Reserve your spot today and enjoy a night that hits all the right notes.
